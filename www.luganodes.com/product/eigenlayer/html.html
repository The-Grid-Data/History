EigenLayer And Restaking

EigenLayer is a restaking protocol on Ethereum, enabling users to restake native ETH or LSTs for projects beyond Ethereum. It extends Ethereum's cryptoeconimic security to diverse protocols/services, granting projects access to its massive staked capital base and validator set.

For Developers

Actively Validated Services (AVSs) on EigenLayer empower developers to tap into Ethereum's security infrastructure for their projects, enhancing security and scalability by leveraging pooled security and immediate community support, without having to bootstrap from scratch.

For Institutions / Users Holding ETH

ETH holders can earn additional yields through ETH re-staking while simultaneously helping the operators to secure other protocols and applications (AVS) within its ecosystem. This innovative approach not only enhances the yield potential for users but also contributes to the security and growth of the broader blockchain ecosystem.
