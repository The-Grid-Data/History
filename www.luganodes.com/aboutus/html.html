About Luganodes

Luganodes is a AAA rated institutional-grade staking service provider and one of the top leaders in staking volume. Globally, Luganodes’s client base stakes digital assets across 46+ PoS live networks with 99.99% uptime. It is one of the top validator on the Polygon network and the super representative on the Tron network.

With Luganodes, enterprises and individuals can take part in validating blockchain transactions and earning staking rewards. We embark on our sophisticated services for corporate clients and individual stakers.
