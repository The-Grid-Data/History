How to Buy Cryptocurrency on the Binance Homepage?

2021-07-30 09:32
There are a number of ways to buy cryptocurrencies from the Binance homepage. You can use a credit/debit card, cash balance, or Apple Pay/Google Pay. Before getting started, please make sure you’ve completed identity verification for your Binance account.
1. Log in to your Binance account and click [Buy Crypto] on the homepage.
2. You can buy crypto with different fiat currencies. Enter the fiat amount you want to spend and the system will automatically display the amount of crypto you can get. In this example, we’ll use 100 EUR to buy BTC.
3. Depending on your country/region, you’ll see different payment options. Click the box under [Pay With] to select a preferred payment method.
Expand the drop-down boxes below to view a step-by-step guide on each payment method.

Buy crypto with credit/debit card

4. Choose [Card] and click [Confirm].
5. If you haven’t purchased crypto with a debit/credit card before, you’ll see that the [Buy] button has changed to [Add New Card]. Click it and enter your card information. Then, click [Confirm].
6. You’ll be redirected to the payment confirmation page. Check the payment details and confirm your order within the time indicated, after which the price and the amount of crypto you can get will be recalculated. To get a new quote, click [Refresh].
Click [Confirm] to place your order.
7. You'll be redirected to your bank's OTP transaction page. Follow the instructions to verify the payment. After that, you’ll see the purchased crypto in your Spot Wallet.

Buy crypto with Fiat Wallet balance

4. Choose [Wallet] and click [Confirm].
5. If you don’t have a sufficient balance in your account, you’ll see an error message. To top up your wallet balance, click [Fiat Center].
5.1. Under the deposit section, choose a deposit option.
5.2. You’ll be redirected to the [Deposit Fiat] page to top up. For more details, please refer to the FAQ articles.
6. Check the payment details and confirm your order within the time indicated, after which the price and the amount of crypto you can get will be recalculated. The system will automatically refresh the quote. Click [Confirm] to place your order.
7. Please wait patiently for the order to process. You’ll see the purchased crypto in your Spot Wallet shortly.

Buy crypto with Apple Pay/Google Pay

4. Choose [Apple Pay] or [Google Pay] and click [Confirm].
5. Click [Buy BTC].
6. Check the payment details and confirm your order within the time indicated, after which the price and the amount of crypto you can get will be recalculated. To get a new quote, click [Refresh].
Click [Confirm] to place your order.
7.1 Apple Pay
If you haven’t added any credit or debit card to your Apple account, you’ll be prompted to do so at this step. Follow the instructions to add a card, then confirm the order with your password or Touch ID.
7.2 Google Pay
You’ll see a pop-up window with your Google account. Enter your credit or debit card details and click [Pay].
8. Please wait patiently for the order to process. You’ll see the purchased crypto in your Spot Wallet shortly.
