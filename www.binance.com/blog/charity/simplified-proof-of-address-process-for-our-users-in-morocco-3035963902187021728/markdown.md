# Simplified Proof of Address Process for Our Users in Morocco

2023-09-15

## Main Takeaways

  * Binance recognizes the challenges in obtaining traditional Proof of Address (POA) documents in Morocco due to the recent tragic earthquake. 

  * Moroccan users can now just upload a clear photo of their ID for POA verification.

  * This change aims to ensure the speedy delivery of financial aid to those in need, and uninterrupted access to Binance services.

_Binance Simplifies POA Verification for Moroccan Users Amid Natural
Disaster._

![](https://public.bnbstatic.com/image/cms/blog/20230915/46e9ae56-7cbc-44fd-968c-47a203fdfc8d)

At Binance, we understand that the recent natural disasters and the ensuing
crisis have made it difficult for many of our users to obtain bank statements
or utility bills, which are typically used to secure Proof of Address (POA)
verification. We acknowledge the challenges you are facing and want to do our
best to lend you immediate support.

In light of these difficult circumstances, we have simplified the POA process
for Binance users in Morocco. Moving forward, all you need to do is upload a
clear photo of the back of your ID card, as long as your name and address are
clearly visible.

This simplified process is aimed at facilitating the speedy delivery of aid to
those in need, and providing our Moroccan users with uninterrupted access to
Binance services, despite the disruptions caused by the natural disasters.

Stay strong, and know that Binance is here for you through these challenging
times!

For anyone wishing to help provide some support during this difficult time,
Binance Charity has launched a public donation address, and we invite everyone
willing to help to contribute digital assets. Every donation, big or small,
goes a long way.

We will donate the total amount received to an authorized NGO to help the
people in need.

[_Donate Here_](https://www.binance.charity/projects/43/Morocco-Earthquake-
Emergency-Appeal)
