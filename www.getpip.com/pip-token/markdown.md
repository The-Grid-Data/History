REWARD FARMING

PIP Token distribution is based on engagements, create new content and get
tips from other PIP user, send money to people, interact with PIP and get
regular rewards of $PIP token.

![](https://assets-global.website-
files.com/61700e28852fe1f60dc6cb9a/624ce9cc742560d91daf36a2_Blockchain%20\(front%20view\)%20\(1\).png)

LIQUIDITY REWARDS

Liquidity providers will gain rewards based on their percentage share of the
overall liquidity pool. Rewards are paid in our native token $PIP to every
liquidity provider that locks liquidity.

![](https://assets-global.website-
files.com/61700e28852fe1f60dc6cb9a/624ce9e0b04ce8a7a011d0ce_Safe%20\(front%20view\).png)

GOVERNANCE

$PIP is the native token of PipDAO. The DAO is the owner of the PIP Ecosystem.
$PIP holders are members of PipDAO and possess ownership of the PIP Ecosystem
through the DAO.
