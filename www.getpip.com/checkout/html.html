Which cryptocurrencies does PIP support for payments?

PIP supports a range of cryptocurrencies for payments. Currently, you can accept the following cryptocurrencies:
- Solana: SOL, PIP, USDC, USDT
- Ethereum: ETH, USDC
- BNB Chain: BNB, BUSD
- Polygon: MATIC, USDC, USDT
- Avalanche: AVAX
With PIP Checkout, you have the flexibility to choose multiple cryptocurrencies you wish to receive as a seller. Please note that PIP has plans to support more blockchain networks in the future to provide even more options for accepting cryptocurrencies.

When using Checkout, fees are calculated as follows:

Product Price + Sales Tax = Total Amount
