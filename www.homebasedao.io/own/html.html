Unlocking Homeownership for Everyone

At Homebase, we believe buying a house shouldn't be limited to just those with enough for a downpayment and good credit score. With a 10% downpayment, we can help you step into a home of your choosing and start building equity right away.

By utilizing the Homebase platform, any house can become a home.

Tell Us About You and Identify a Property

Start by submitting information about yourself and identify a home that interests you, either in your backyard or in an area that you've dreamed of living in.

Receive a Proposal from Homebase

Homebase evaluates the price of the home using our underwriting and data partners, and will send a proposal to you in just a few days time.

Become an Ownership Partner

After approval, we will begin the purchasing, financing, and closing process, ending with you becoming the champion of a new Homebase home.
