# Scaling Tokenization and Building Interoperability in the Primary and
Secondary Markets

##### Report

Download Report

The value of tokenized US treasury assets on public blockchains like Ethereum
and Polygon has surpassed $650 million in August 2023, up from a $100 million
in January the same year. Hong Kong just completed issuance of tokenized green
bonds worth $100 million. And Northern Trust just announced a new distributed
ledger technology (DLT) project that enables tokenization of carbon credits.

These are however only notional wins when compared with the predictions around
the total potential of tokenization, predicted to be in the $20 trillion range
by 2030 by the likes of Boston Consulting Group and Citi.
