FAQ

What is the Basis Trade Engine?

The Basis Trade Engine (BTE) provides Basis NFT holders with real-time data on funding & lending rate data from 12 top exchanges. It highlights the most favourable basis trade opportunities currently available, with the advanced analysis needed to interpret and put these opportunities into action.

What is the Basis Trade eXecutor?

The Basis Trade eXecutor (BTX) is a proprietary trade execution platform that links to your favourite exchanges via API. It provides users with the guidance and tools to open top performing delta-neutral trades with ease, avoiding any errors with manual execution.
