StakeNet

StakeNet is a self-sustaining, transparent, and decentralized protocol for operating intelligent stake pools.

With StakeNet, protocols have the power to fully decentralize stake pool operations and live forever. They can leverage on-chain governance to transparently modify parameters and state machine behavior instead of relying on centralized operators for management.
