## Hi-Fi for DeFi

Pyth connects high-fidelity market data from the world’s largest professional
traders and exchanges to any smart contract, anywhere.

![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Forb.cd53afc9.png&w=3840&q=75)

## Solving DeFi’s oracle problem

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%27393%27/%3e)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Foracle.4ca17044.svg&w=828&q=75)

### Decentralized finance relies on high-fidelity, time-sensitive, real-world
data, but has no way to access it.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%27393%27/%3e)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fsolution.7f243939.svg&w=828&q=75)

### We deliver a decentralized, cross-chain market of verifiable data from
first-party sources.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%27393%27/%3e)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fresult.3db86a42.svg&w=828&q=75)

### We’re giving DeFi comprehensive access to the vast amount of hi-fi
financial services data it needs to make smart contracts work smarter.
