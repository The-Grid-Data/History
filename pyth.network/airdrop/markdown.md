## What is the  Airdrop Program

The Pyth Network Retrospective Airdrop is for community members who
contributed to the Pyth ecosystem, culture, and community.

This program will distribute PYTH Tokens to active community members and DeFi
participants who used decentralized applications powered by Pyth data. The
airdrop is retrospective, meaning only individuals who contributed in the past
will be eligible to claim tokens.

[read blog](https://pyth.network/blog/pyth-network-retrospective-airdrop)

## PYTH  Airdrop Process

This quick guide will walk you through the Claim Process for the Pyth Network
Retrospective Airdrop.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%2792%27/%3e)![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fractangle.5967306b.png&w=828&q=75)

1Welcome

The Claim Process will check your wallet activity and or Discord account to
calculate how many tokens you are eligible to claim.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%2792%27/%3e)![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fractangle.5967306b.png&w=828&q=75)

2Review Airdrop Eligibility

Select the Pyth-supported ecosystems you are active in. Select the Pyth
Discord checkbox if relevant.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%2792%27/%3e)![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fractangle.5967306b.png&w=828&q=75)

3Verify Eligibility

Connect your wallets. Log in to your Discord account if relevant. Your token
allocation will be displayed per connected wallet or account.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%2792%27/%3e)![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fractangle.5967306b.png&w=828&q=75)

4Log in with Solana

PYTH is native to Solana. Connect your Solana wallet if you have not done so
already. The Claim Page will help you create a new wallet. The wallet you
connect in this step will receive your allocation.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%2792%27/%3e)![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fractangle.5967306b.png&w=828&q=75)

5Claim Tokens

Click the corresponding “sign” button for each wallet. Your wallet(s) will ask
if you wish to sign the transaction. Confirm by clicking “sign” in your
wallet’s pop-up window.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3csvg%20xmlns=%27http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%27%20version=%271.1%27%20width=%27393%27%20height=%2792%27/%3e)![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)![](/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fractangle.5967306b.png&w=828&q=75)

6Next Steps

You will receive your allocation in your connected Solana wallet. You can
share your experience and participate in Pyth Governance. You can repeat the
Claim Process.
