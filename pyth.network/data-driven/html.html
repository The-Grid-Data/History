What is Data Driven 2023?

Data Driven 2023 brings together developers, blockchain users, data experts, financial institutions, and governance participants of the Pyth Network oracle solution.

Contributors to Pyth and the wider Web3 ecosystem will shine a light on the current state of blockchain technology, the emerging era of high-throughput decentralized finance, and how Pyth can continue transforming the ways we transact and trade.

Who is Pyth

The Pyth Network is the largest first-party oracle for the world’s financial data. It supports more than 300 real-time price feeds across digital assets, equities, ETFs, FX, and commodities.

The network comprises some of the world's largest exchanges, market makers, and financial services providers contributing their proprietary price data on-chain for aggregation and distribution to smart contracts.
