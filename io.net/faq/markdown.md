What is io.net mission, and what are you working towards?

![](/_next/static/media/navArrow.84dd333a.svg)

io.net is a decentralized GPU network designed to give unlimited computing
power to ML applications. We make computing more scalable, accessible, and
efficient. Our mission is to unlock fair access to computing power by
assembling 1 million + GPUs from independent data centers, crypto miners, and
crypto projects such as Filecoin or Render.

How is io.net different from AWS?

![](/_next/static/media/navArrow.84dd333a.svg)

io.net offers a fundamentally different approach to cloud computing,
leveraging a distributed and decentralized model, which can provide more
control and flexibility to users, our services are permissionless and cost
efficient. The combination of all these factors sets io.net in its own league
of Decentralized providers.

What is a DePIN and how does io.net fit?

![](/_next/static/media/navArrow.84dd333a.svg)

DePIN, or Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks, leverages
blockchains, IoT and the greater Web3 ecosystem to create, operate and
maintain real-world physical infrastructure. These networks leverage token
incentives to coordinate, reward and safeguard members of the network. io.net
is the first and only GPU DePIN. We are optimized for machine learning but
suitable for all GPU use cases as we connect computing power providers with
users to offer accessibility and profit for everyone involved.
