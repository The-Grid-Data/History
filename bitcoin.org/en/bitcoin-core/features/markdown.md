## Full Validation

Bitcoin Core ensures every block and transaction it accepts is valid,
increasing not only your security but also **helping prevent miners and banks
from taking control of Bitcoin.**

[Learn about full validation](/en/bitcoin-core/features/validation)

![icon](/img/icons/ico_control.svg?1716491272)

## Better Privacy

Bitcoin Core provides **exclusive privacy features** that can make it hard for
anyone to link you to your transactions.

[Discover the privacy advantages](/en/bitcoin-core/features/privacy)

![icon](/img/icons/ico_better_security.svg?1716491272)
