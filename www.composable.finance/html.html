Chain-agnostic execution

Composable's MANTIS - Multichain Agnostic Normalized Trust-Minimized Intent Settlement - empowers users to seamlessly define and execute their intents across any blockchain, abstracting cross-chain complexities, all while harnessing the security of the IBC protocol.
