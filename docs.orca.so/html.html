Introduction

Welcome to Orca: Solana's apex liquidity layer.

Orca (https://www.orca.so) is an advanced protocol built around a Concentrated Liquidity Automated Market Maker (CLAMM). If you want to know more you can dive into Orca: find out more - or explore documents tailored to your interests (see links below). If you're feeling chatty swim over to the Discord community pages to engage with fellow podmates, or to ask a friendly Community Manager for support.
