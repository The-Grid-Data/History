        How it works

        You can activate Rewards with one click in the Kraken or Kraken Pro app or website. Earnings are then generated by flex staking a portion of your eligible assets on-chain or allocating to our Opt-In Rewards program*. The APR rates shown are before applicable fees are deducted and are an estimate of rewards you could receive on the assets you hold.

        Your assets can be traded, withdrawn or used for margin trading** collateral at any time, there’s no lockup period.

        FAQs

        1. Who can use Rewards? 

          You! If you have a verified account in a supported location and own eligible assets, you can start using Rewards. Your earnings will begin growing in your account the next day. 

        2. How do I turn Rewards on or off? 

          On the Kraken app or website, access your Account balance page and check lifetime rewards. From here you can turn Rewards on or off anytime.

          On the Kraken Pro app or website, access your Portfolio page to turn Rewards on. To turn it off access Settings on web or Account details on the app to turn Rewards off.

        3. How much can I earn?

          Each eligible crypto asset has its own estimated APR (Annual Percentage Return). Check our list of eligible assets to see the APR for each asset.

        4. When are my earnings paid out?

          Rewards accumulate every day and all your earnings are paid out on a weekly basis. Rewards are paid in the same asset that earned them.

        5. What are the limits?

          You can earn rewards for any eligible asset with a balance of more than 1 USD. The total amount of each asset eligible for Rewards is capped. Asset caps can be seen here. There is no limit to the amount of rewards that can be earned by eligible assets. 

        6. Where can I see my rewards?

          On the Kraken app or web, access your Account balance page and check lifetime Rewards.

          On the Kraken Pro app or web go to Portfolio, Spot to see Total Spot rewards.

        7. What is staking?

          Staking allows individuals to earn rewards by contributing to the security and decentralization of the blockchain network utilizing blockchain Proof-of-Stake protocol.

        8. What are Opt-In Rewards?

          Opt-In Rewards gives you the option to earn rewards on available and idle Bitcoin (BTC), USD Coin (USDC) and Tether (USDT) balances in your Kraken account. Opt-In Rewards utilizes assets as further described in our Terms of Service.

        9. Can I use Rewards both in the regular and the Pro apps?

          Yes, Rewards can be enabled in either app or website and will only be active on assets not already staked in Pro.

        10. Is there a fee for using Rewards?

          No, we don’t charge any additional fee, however Kraken does charge a commission on the rewards generated. See here for more details.

        11. Do I need to pay tax on rewards generated by Rewards?

          Tax may need to be paid in some locations. We suggest you speak to a tax professional for accurate, local advice.
