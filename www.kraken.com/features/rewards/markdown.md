Rewards is a simple way to grow your crypto holdings with no additional
effort. By turning on Rewards you can generate earnings with no lock-up
periods, and your funds are always accessible. It's the easy way to get more
out of your crypto.

Rewards is not available to US and UK clients.

[Start earning](https://kraken.app.link/rewards-signup)

## How it works

You can activate Rewards with one click in the Kraken or Kraken Pro app or
website. Earnings are then generated by flex staking a portion of your
eligible assets on-chain or allocating to our Opt-In Rewards program*. [The
APR rates](https://support.kraken.com/hc/articles/overview-of-rewards-on-
kraken) shown are before applicable fees are deducted and are an estimate of
rewards you could receive on the assets you hold.

Always under your control

Your assets can be traded, withdrawn or used for [margin
trading](/features/margin-trading)** collateral at any time, there’s no lockup
period.

Earn in the assets you hold

Whether you have Ether, Polkadot,  
Cardano or any other, your weekly earnings will be paid out in that asset.

Your assets grow automatically

Your weekly earnings are automatically  
added to your balances and compound  
over time.

Optimize the performance of your portfolio

With a single touch, generate passive income from assets that traditionally
may be lying idle.

* Allocation depends on asset types. Opt-In Rewards is available in select geos only.  
** Geo restrictions apply.

## FAQs

  1. ### **Who can use Rewards?  **

You! If you have a verified account in a supported location and own eligible
assets, you can start using Rewards. Your earnings will begin growing in your
account the next day.

* * *

  2. ### **How do I turn Rewards on or off?  **

On the Kraken app or website, access your Account balance page and check
lifetime rewards. From here you can turn Rewards on or off anytime.

On the Kraken Pro app or website, access your Portfolio page to turn Rewards
on. To turn it off access Settings on web or Account details on the app to
turn Rewards off.

* * *

  3. ### **How much can I earn?**

Each eligible crypto asset has its own estimated APR (Annual Percentage
Return). Check our [list of eligible
assets](https://support.kraken.com/hc/articles/overview-of-rewards-on-kraken)
to see the APR for each asset.

* * *

  4. ### **When are my earnings paid out?**

Rewards accumulate every day and all your earnings are paid out on a weekly
basis. Rewards are paid in the same asset that earned them.

* * *

  5. ### **What are the limits?**

You can earn rewards for any eligible asset with a balance of more than 1 USD.
The total amount of each asset eligible for Rewards is capped. Asset caps can
be seen [here](https://support.kraken.com/hc/articles/overview-of-rewards-on-
kraken). There is no limit to the amount of rewards that can be earned by
eligible assets.

* * *

  6. ### **Where can I see my rewards?**

On [the Kraken app](/features/cryptocurrency-apps) or web, access your Account
balance page and check lifetime Rewards.

On [the Kraken Pro](https://pro.kraken.com/app/) app or web go to Portfolio,
Spot to see Total Spot rewards.

* * *

  7. ### **What is staking?**

Staking allows individuals to earn rewards by contributing to the security and
decentralization of the blockchain network utilizing blockchain [Proof-of-
Stake protocol](/learn/proof-of-work-vs-proof-of-stake).

* * *

  8. ### **What are Opt-In Rewards?**

Opt-In Rewards gives you the option to earn rewards on available and idle
Bitcoin (BTC), USD Coin (USDC) and Tether (USDT) balances in your Kraken
account. Opt-In Rewards utilizes assets as further described in our [Terms of
Service](/legal).

* * *

  9. ### **Can I use Rewards both in the regular and the Pro apps?**

Yes, Rewards can be enabled in either app or website and will only be active
on assets not already staked in [Pro](https://pro.kraken.com/).

* * *

  10. ### **Is there a fee for using Rewards?**

No, we don’t charge any additional fee, however Kraken does charge a
commission on the rewards generated. See
[here](https://support.kraken.com/hc/articles/overview-of-rewards-on-kraken)
for more details.

* * *

  11. ### **Do I need to pay tax on rewards generated by Rewards?**

Tax may need to be paid in some locations. We suggest you speak to a tax
professional for accurate, local advice.

* * *
