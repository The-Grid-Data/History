        Crypto converter FAQ:

        1. What does conversion rate mean?

          Conversion rate is the amount of an asset you can receive for each unit of another asset.

        2. What affects a conversion rate?

          The conversion rate between assets may fluctuate based on supply and demand, relative scarcity, investor sentiment, media attention and other factors.

        3. How do I use this cryptocurrency converter tool?

          Enter the amount of an asset you wish to convert to another asset in the first field. The crypto converter will automatically calculate the amount you will recieve for trading a crypto for another crypto. You can also trade fiat for crypto and crypto for fiat

        4. How can I convert crypto?

          1. Ensure the cryptocurrencies you want to convert are selected in the converter tool
          2. Enter the amount of an asset you want to convert into another asset
          3. Click "Trade now" to sign up or sign into your Kraken account
          4. Convert assets with just a few clicks

        5. Can I buy cryptocurrency with fiat?

          Yes, crypto to fiat trading pairs are available on Kraken.
