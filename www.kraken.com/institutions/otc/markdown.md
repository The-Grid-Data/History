## OTC

  1. ### What is the OTC Portal?

The [OTC Portal](https://pro.kraken.com/app/otc/quote) is a self-service
system that enables OTC clients to get executable, automated quotes for
supported cryptoassets, including:

     * Customized price charts based on your trading habits
     * Position exposure information
     * Recent transactions and OTC trading history
     * RFQ access

* * *

  2. ### What cryptocurrencies do we support?

**Trade over chat:**

     * All [asset pairs listed](https://support.kraken.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000678446-Cryptocurrencies-available-on-Kraken) on the Kraken exchange (subject to geographic [restrictions](https://support.kraken.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001368823-Geographic-Restrictions-Can-I-use-Kraken-if-I-m-from-))

**RFQ** :

     * Over [20 assets, including stablecoins](https://support.kraken.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021967711) (subject to geographic [restrictions](https://support.kraken.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001368823-Geographic-Restrictions-Can-I-use-Kraken-if-I-m-from-))

**OTC Derivatives:**

     * BTC and ETH are supported for options trading (subject to geographic [restrictions](https://support.kraken.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001368823-Geographic-Restrictions-Can-I-use-Kraken-if-I-m-from-)). 
     * OTC Derivatives services are only available to OTC clients in certain jurisdictions. [Learn more](https://support.kraken.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021967711-Introducing-the-Kraken-OTC-Desk#:~:text=conduct%20large%20orders.-,OTC%20derivatives,-%3A) about OTC Derivatives.

* * *

  3. ### What is the minimum trade size?

The minimum trade amount is an equivalent value of $100,000 USD. There is no
maximum size for trading over chat.  

* * *

  4. ### Is there a fee?

Kraken OTC does not charge any additional trading fees. The bid or offer price
we show is the 'all-inclusive' price.

  6. ### 

#####  How does settlement work?

     * Trade obligations must be settled within 24 hours
     * Crypto can be settled to/from your Kraken exchange wallet or sent to the external wallet of your choice
     * USD can be settled to/from your Kraken account or wired directly to your bank account
     * AUD can be settled directly to/from your domestic bank account
     * Other fiat currencies available for trading on Kraken must be settled to your Kraken account



Your settlement options:

     * Flexible settlement arrangements allow you to settle trades with your Kraken account, your bank, or an external wallet of your choice within 24 hours
     * [RFQ](https://pro.kraken.com/app/otc/quote) offers a fully automated trade and settlement feature, in addition to flexible settlement (subject to eligibility): 
       * **Automated settlement** allows you to trade and settle with your Kraken balance. Your Kraken account must be funded, and once your trade is filled your assets will be settled into your Kraken account within seconds.
       * **Flexible settlement** , available to eligible clients, allows you to settle trades within 24 hours via your Kraken account, bank or an external wallet.

* * *

### Terms and eligibility

  * We require a minimum order size of $100,000 and will consider exceptions on a case-by-case basis.

  * Eligibility for OTC trading is subject to AML/KYC and other requirements.

  * Kraken OTC does not hold custody of assets on behalf of trading counterparties.

  * Kraken OTC is available to Pro-level verified clients.
