institutional traders

### Fee rebates

Enjoy fee rebates on top markets.

### Multi-collateral futures

Leverage across your portfolio. Take profits in the asset of your choice.

Trade on our fully compliant Kraken MTF.

## Designed for institutions

### Subaccounts

Trade and manage multiple trading strategies separately, by adding
subaccounts.

### Real-time market data

Historical and real-time market data for research and backtesting. L3 and
enriched data available.

whitelisting.
