One of the most ambitious cryptocurrencies to date, Ethereum was launched in

XRP is a cryptocurrency that aims to complement traditional payments,

### Synthetix (SNX)

Synthetix is a software that allows users to mint new crypto assets that mimic
both real-world assets (like the U.S. dollar) and crypto assets (like
Bitcoin). These synthetic assets (Synths) are backed by the platform's
cryptocurrency, Synthetix Network Token (SNX), which is staked as collateral
in order to generate rewards.

[How to Buy](/learn/buy-synthetix-snx)[How to Sell](/learn/sell-synthetix-snx)

[![bal](https://assets.kraken.com/marketing/web/icons/sym-
bal_colored.svg)](/learn/what-is-balancer-bal)

### Balancer (BAL)

Balancer facilitates trading without a central order book, instead letting
users create index funds by locking up as many as eight crypto assets into
custom lending pools. With Balancer, your portfolio is rebalanced on the
blockchain by traders who are taking advantage of arbitrage opportunities.

[How to Buy](/learn/buy-balancer-bal)[How to Sell](/learn/sell-balancer-bal)

[![crv](https://assets.kraken.com/marketing/web/icons/sym-
crv_colored.svg)](/learn/what-is-curve-crv)

### Curve (CRV)

Curve awards the CRV token to users who provide liquidity to their pools.
Traders then use the pooled funds to exchange different stablecoins, avoiding
slippage and high fees.

[How to Buy](/learn/buy-curve-crv)[How to Sell](/learn/sell-curve-crv)

[![dot](https://assets.kraken.com/marketing/web/icons/sym-
dot_colored.svg)](/learn/what-is-polkadot-dot)

### Polkadot (DOT)

Polkadot is a software that seeks to incentivize a global network of computers
to operate a blockchain on top of which users can launch and operate their own
blockchains.

[How to Buy](/learn/buy-polkadot-dot)[How to Sell](/learn/sell-polkadot-dot)

### Kusama (KSM)

Kusama is a public pre-production environment for Polkadot, one that allows
any developer to experiment and test new blockchains or applications before
releasing them on this network.

[How to Buy](/learn/buy-kusama-ksm)[How to Sell](/learn/sell-kusama-ksm)

rep_colored.svg)](/learn/what-is-augur-rep)

### Augur (REP)

Augur is a software aiming to incentivize a global network of computers to
maintain a prediction market platform on top of Ethereum (ETH). Users can
state opinions on the likelihood of a future event by buying shares in a given
outcome without reliance on a managing bookkeeper.

[How to Buy](/learn/buy-augur-rep)[How to Sell](/learn/sell-augur-rep)
