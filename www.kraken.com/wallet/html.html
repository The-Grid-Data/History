        Kraken Wallet FAQs

        1. How do I back up my Kraken Wallet?

          Write down your Secret Recovery Phrase and store in a secure location.

        2. Is Kraken Wallet free to use?

          Yes, Kraken Wallet is free to use, and available on both iOS and Android supported devices.

        3. What blockchains does Kraken Wallet initially support?

          Kraken Wallet supports Bitcoin, Ethereum mainnet, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Solana, Dogecoin and Base.

        4. Is Kraken Wallet open-source?

          Yes, it is open-sourced, ensuring transparency and community contribution.
